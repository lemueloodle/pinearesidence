Pinea
Standing atop the hills of Ballouneh, Pinea is a gated community stretching over 17,000 sqm, offering 232 simplexes and duplexes ranging from 110 to 220 sqm and built around a central common garden that brings the residents together in a community-like atmosphere.
The landspaced greenery within reflects the outside natural beauty that distinguishes the region, a beauty that extends to prime vistas of the sea and the mountains. The green surroundings and fresh mountain air are complemented by urban comforts and conveniences that provide the project with the best of both lifestyles, an ideal image of life on the Lebanese hillside.
